
Through my study of Cyber Crime, I was amazed at the simplicity of how easily people can be taken advantage of by UPI scams through small mistakes, such as sharing their OTPs or authorising unknown requests. I have found that cyber criminals often use psychological manipulation rather than technical hacking methods.

One thing I will be changing in my own habits is to be more cautious with how I deal with digital payments. I will always verify requests before authorising transactions, as well as not clicking on unknown links. This assignment has helped to raise my level of understanding regarding the risks of the internet and the issues surrounding Cyber Security in everyday life.
