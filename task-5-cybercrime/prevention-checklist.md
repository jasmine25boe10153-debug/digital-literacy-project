# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.
2. Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps.
3. Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or services.
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links received via SMS, WhatsApp, or email.
5. Use official apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store.
6. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts.
7. Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history.
8. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
9. Install antivirus software and keep your phone updated.
10. Be cautious of offers that seem “too good to be true.”

## Financial / UPI Safety Tips

- Always double-check the recipient’s UPI ID before sending money.
- Remember: Receiving money does NOT require entering your UPI PIN.
