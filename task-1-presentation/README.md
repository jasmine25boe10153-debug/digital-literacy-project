Using Canva, a user-friendly graphic design program, I developed an engaging infographic which represented Digital Literacy, Safe Internet Practices and Email Etiquette in an organized manner. By utilizing its templates, icons, text styles and color palette features allowed me to create a visually appealing and informative.
I utilized a consistent and simple design and used contrasting text combined with a limited number of colors to provide visual clarity when reading my information. I used bullet points as a means of easy identification, as well as pictures, to create a more appealing and relatable design for students.
Additionally, I found the way in which small design attributes such as spacing between letters will determine the way in which a user perceives the data. As a result, I struggled to ensure the text and visual elements of the infographic did not create a feeling of being overcrowded. Overall, through completion of this task of the project, I improved my digital design skills and my understanding of digital literacy concepts.
