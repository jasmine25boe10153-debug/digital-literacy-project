✔️ Do’s

•Think before posting — ensure content is respectful and appropriate

•Maintain privacy settings and protect personal information

•Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation

•Use professional language when representing yourself publicly

•Be respectful in comments and discussions

❌ Don’ts

•Do not share sensitive personal or academic information

•Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content

•Do not engage in online arguments or trolling

•Avoid oversharing your location or daily activities

•Do not plagiarize or repost content without credit
